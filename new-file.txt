Padma awardee Karimul Haque, also known as 'Bike Ambulance Dada' met PM Modi upon his arrival at Bagdogra Airport, in 2021.
Haque was awarded Padma award for his unique way of social service by ferrying patients to medical facilities on his motorbike in Jalpaiguri Dist
Modi embraced him and hugged him with affection for what he is doing.
It is an initiative of Modi government to seek out such unknown people who are rendering exemplary social services for recognition and Padma award.
Karimul was also part of “Kaun Banega Crorepati” episode (Season-12, episode-70) where megastar Amitabh Bachchan recognized his social service.
